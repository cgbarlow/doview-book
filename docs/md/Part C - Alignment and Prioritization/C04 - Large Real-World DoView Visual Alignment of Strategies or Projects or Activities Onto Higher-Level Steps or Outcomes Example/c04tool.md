# DoView Tool C4 — Large Real-World DoView Visual Alignment of Strategies/Projects/Activities Onto Higher-Level Steps/Outcomes Example

> **Pair:** [Question](c04question.md) · Tool (this page)

The DoView strategy/outcomes diagram below shows how linking between strategies/activities/projects and higher-level steps and outcomes is scalable when using suitable apps or platforms\*.

## Diagram

The page is a real-world poster-style example: on the left, a series of stacked panels each containing dense matrices of strategy/activity/project boxes; on the right, a corresponding series of higher-level drill-down step/outcome panels (color-coded green, purple, yellow, brown, red). Hundreds of fine line-links fan from the project boxes on the left to the step/outcome boxes on the right, demonstrating large-scale 'line-of-sight' alignment in a single visualization.

_This page contains a real-world poster image of many interconnected DoView pages with hundreds of line-links; it does not translate cleanly to Mermaid and is described above._

\* For instance the free DoView legacy app.

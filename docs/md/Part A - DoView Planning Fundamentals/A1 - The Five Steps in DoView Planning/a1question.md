# How can DoView strategy/outcomes diagrams be used for any government, corporate, nonprofit or community organizational or initiative planning?

> **Pair:** Question (this page) · [Tool](a1tool.md)

The DoView Planning approach is a simple planning process based on using DoView strategy/outcomes diagrams. Use it at any level within any organization, agency, provider, policy or initiative. It has five generic steps: A) show your proposed outcomes and steps in a DoView drill-down strategy/outcomes diagram; B) focus your activity on your priorities (show DoView Visual Alignment); C) measure and set accountabilities; D) evaluate success and impact; and, E) dashboard results onto the DoView strategy/outcomes diagram to report on and improve performance.

These steps can be used in different combinations to complement when you are undertaking typical organizational planning and related functions. These include: co-design; strategic planning and priority setting; organizational alignment/enterprise portfolio management; program monitoring/performance management; program evaluation; evidence-based practice; collective impact; and, outcomes-focused contracting/delegation.

At the current time, many organizations undertaken these different functions somewhat separately. However, to do each of these functions, you must first start with listing the outcomes being sought, and the steps that it is believed will lead to them. Currently, this listing of outcomes and steps is sometimes done separately for each of the different organizational functions above. Then you can end up with outcomes and steps represented in somewhat different ways. In addition, not all of the lists may be exactly the same. Then someone in the organization has to go through the different lists of outcomes and steps to make sure that they are all in alignment and agreement with each other.

It is much more efficient to just do the outcomes specification once for the organization or initiative using a standard format - a DoView strategy/outcomes diagram. Once you have done this ('A' in the tool on the next page), you can then use this same DoView strategy diagram as the basis for all of the different organizational functions listed within the tool. This automatically helps you ensure alignment between the different organizational or initiative functions.

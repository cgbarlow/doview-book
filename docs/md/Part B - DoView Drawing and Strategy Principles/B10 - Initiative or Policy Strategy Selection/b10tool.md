# DoView Tool B10 — Initiative or Policy Strategy Selection

> **Pair:** [Question](b10question.md) · Tool (this page)

## Approach × Method × Style

| Approach | Method | Style |
|---|---|---|
| Educational / Informational | One-to-one; Classroom/Group; Social Media Campaigns; Traditional Media Campaigns | Knowledge acquisition; Attitude change; Behavior change; Increasing awareness of the situation and rights |
| Community Mobilization | Group formation; Use of natural community leaders; Grass roots community workers; Community organizers/activists; Provision of resources to communities | Community support; Voicing community concerns; Co-production; Real change on the ground |
| Monitoring and Regulatory Enforcement | Information provision about requirements; Monitoring compliance with requirements; Public rewarding and shaming ('League Tables'); Monitoring and enforcement | Raising awareness of regulatory requirements; Increasing system responsiveness; Proactive enforcement |
| Institutional Development | Liaison; Sectoral coordination; Intersectoral collaboration | Networking; Coordination; Restructuring/building new institutional infrastructure |
| Policy, Rules and Standards Development | Organizational policies, local policies, by-laws; National policies, regulations, legislation | Identifying policy options; Providing information about policy options; Generating debate on policy options; Coalitions to support new positive policy options |

## Focus / Setting

- Individuals
- Specific Sub-Populations
- Indigenous Peoples
- Ethnic Groups
- Geographic Communities
- Community organizations
- Interest Groups
- Private Companies
- Public Institutions
- Regulatory Authorities
- Central and Local Policy Makers
- Central and Local Politicians

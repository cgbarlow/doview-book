# DoView Tool B11 — Collateral Typically Accompanying a DoView Strategy/Outcomes Diagram

> **Pair:** [Question](b11question.md) · Tool (this page)

There is a standard set of collateral that usually accompanies a DoView strategy/outcomes diagram. The major elements in this are:

- **DoView Booklet** — printed booklet containing the one or more DoView strategy/outcomes diagrams developed for the organization or initiative.
- **A clickable drill-down webpage version** of the DoView strategy/outcomes diagram on an intranet or publicly available on the internet using Google Slides, PowerPoint or the DoView Planning legacy app.
- **Printed poster(s)** of the DoView strategy/outcomes diagrams providing an overview of all of the diagrams in the DoView Booklet for quickly overviewing them.
- **Documentation** regarding how the DoView strategy/outcomes diagrams have been built and the relevant DoView Planning tools being used (e.g. including the relevant tools referrenced from this book) with acknowledgment to this book and a link to DoViewPlanning.Org for more information.
- **An electronic version** of the DoView strategy/outcomes diagrams in a suitable app.
- **Links to the relevant DoView subpages** from any online Dashboards created to summarize indicators measuring boxes from the relevant DoView strategy/outcomes diagram subpage.

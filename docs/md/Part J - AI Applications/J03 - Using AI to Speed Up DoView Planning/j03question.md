# How can AI be leveraged to speed up DoView Planning?

> **Pair:** Question (this page) · [Tool](j03tool.md)

In essence, DoView Planning is about getting in front of decision makers a clear consensus version of the 'This-Then' logic of any strategy, policy or initiative. They can use the DoView diagram that sets this out as a 'shared thinking tool' and go on to utilize DoView Planning's tools to translate into action boxes in the diagram they regard as priorities. Importantly, because DoView Planning is an applied version of outcomes theory, they can do this without encountering the technical problems that plague outcomes-based planning (e.g. issues such as attribution and accountability).

While aspects of DoView Planning have been applied with many initiatives in many sectors, there have been bottlenecks to its wider application. These have included: developing fit-for-purpose DoView diagrams that follow the DoView Drawing Rules (B7), quickly thinking up indicators for the boxes within a DoView, and mapping projects/activities onto the steps/outcomes within a DoView using DoView Visual Alignment. Previously, to undertake these DoView Planning steps required experts, and the cost and scarcity of them have limited the use of DoView Planning.

The introduction of AI changes this. For instance, using our AI DoView Drawing Prompt, in about ten minutes you can produce a PowerPoint version of a DoView diagram for any organization or initiative that conforms to the DoView Drawing Rules (B7). You can upload this PowerPoint to Google Slides and within minutes have a navigable DoView online that colleagues, funders, and stakeholders can explore wherever they are, on any device, with no extra software. You can get the AI DoView Drawing Prompt from DoViewPlanning.Org.

At the moment, we are testing and using the DoView Planning AI prompts that we are developing with OpenAI's o3 because it is a reasoning model and can produce PowerPoint files for download. However, you can try our prompts on any AI system as all systems are progressively getting new features over time. Of course, as with any AI system, what is produced has to be carefully checked by a human. But the use of AI in DoView Planning is transforming the ease with which it can be used with any organization or initiative of any size.

# Conclusion

The DoView Planning approach is an innovative way of planning, implementing, managing performance, assessing impact, and reporting, which enhances efficiency and effectiveness in any organization, strategy, policy or initiative. It is based on two foundations. First is a set of concepts from strategy psychologist Dr Paul Duignan's outcomes theory. As a result, all DoView Planning tools are integrated and conceptually sound. Without a clear conceptual framework for planning, implementation, measurement and reporting, issues such as attribution and accountability, lead to confusion.

Second, the approach is based on using interactive drill-down DoView strategy/outcomes diagrams setting out in an accessible visual form the 'This-Then' logic of what is being attempted. From a strategy psychology perspective, DoView diagrams act as shared thinking tools, reducing cognitive load and extracting decision-makers' mental models of outcomes and the steps required to achieve them.

The approach has been developed and trialed in almost all sectors and with many types of organizations, strategies, policies and initiatives. The tools, checklists, explainers, and examples in this book provide a comprehensive toolkit. Since the tools are integrated, you avoid rework because DoView Planning lets you immediately leverage work you have done with one tool when using another tool.

DoView Planning provides the tools needed for an innovative, comprehensive, efficient, and effective planning approach. To implement DoView Planning in any organization or initiative all you need to do is to apply the tools in this toolkit. You are free to use them in any context just as long as you acknowledge their use to this book and to the website DoViewPlanning.Org.

The advent of AI now makes many aspects of DoView Planning much easier. For example, using our AI DoView Drawing Prompt, robust DoView diagrams can be produced in minutes. We are currently developing new ways that using AI can speed up DoView Planning. Look at our website DoViewPlanning.Org.

As the use of DoView Planning spreads we want to hear from you about your experiences, get in touch via DoViewPlanning.Org.
